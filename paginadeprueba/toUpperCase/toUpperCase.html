toUpperCase


You will be able to transform texts in lower case to upper case and vice versa.



The only thing you have to do is make a variable with some lower case text in it, then write this 'var nameofvar=nameofvar.toUpperCase();', you have transform text that was in lower case in upper case with the propery '.toUpperCase'.









You can do the same but to lower case 'var nameofvar=nameofvar.toLowerCase();'.
