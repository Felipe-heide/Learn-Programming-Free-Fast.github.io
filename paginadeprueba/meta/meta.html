I will show you how make a 'meta' box, this box goes in the 'head' box.


It is used to tell google or another web searcher what your webpage is about. It also makes your text, ponctuation and accentuation look normal.





This is how you code it:

You open the box '<', you write 'meta', you then put 'name="Type of meta"', finally 'content="what is the value of that meta"/>':







There are 3 impotant types of 'meta', the first one is 'name="description"' is to tell google what your webpage is about 'content="Teach people how to program"'. The second one is 'name="author"', content="Felipe Heide". If your webpage has copyrights you can put 'name="copyrights"', 'description="Felipe Heide". There is also the 'name="keywords"' in 'content' you have to put the keywords of your webpage.










There is one last meta I want to show you, it doesn't have the attribute 'name' or 'content', instead it has an attribute called 'charset="utf-8"' it makes calligrafy normal, you have to put this on avery code you make.
