Porcentages


I will show you how to use porcentages to determine the length of a box height, width, margin, border etc.




The value is a porcentage beetween 0 and 100, 100 ocupying the intire length of the page and zero beeing nothing, it looks like this 'width:50%', this will ocupy half the space of a webpage/body or the section it is in.
