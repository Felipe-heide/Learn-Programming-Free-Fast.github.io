Section

I will show you what a section is and how to program it.


This box goes in the 'body' box, it is used to put boxes in it like (buttons, h1s, videos, images, paragraphs, etc). It will be usefull for the design of your webpage.




This is how you code it:

You write '<', 'section>', '<', /section', '>'.



Between the opening of the box (<>) and the closure of the box (< />) you put other boxes. One section can contein a lot of sections.
