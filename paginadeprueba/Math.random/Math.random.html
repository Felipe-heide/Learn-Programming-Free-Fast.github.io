Math.random


This will get a random number, there are three ways of doing it, the first one is this one 'var number=Math.random();' this will get a number beetween 1 and 0 it could be a very long number, '0.16023389650641562'. The second one is this one 'var number=Math.random() * (max - min) + min;', min is equal to the minimum number that the computer has to give you and max is equal to the maximum number, you have to put two times the min value. The last one is this one 'var number=Math.round(Math.random() * (max - min)) + min;' this will get you integer numbers not long numbers, you have to put the second code and beetween parentheses and write 'Math.round.
