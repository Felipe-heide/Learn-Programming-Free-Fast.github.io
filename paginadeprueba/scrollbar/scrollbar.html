Scrollbar


I will show you how to change the style of a scrollbar.



The selector works like this ' selector ::-webkit-scrollbar{properties}', there are 2 other types of selectors for the thumb and the track of the scrollbar, ' selector ::-webkit-scrollbar-thumb{properties}' and ' selector ::-webkit-scrollbar-track{properties}'.







The properties can be 'background-color', 'border/border-radius' and 'width', you can put the background color at 'transparent' in the scrollbar track.
