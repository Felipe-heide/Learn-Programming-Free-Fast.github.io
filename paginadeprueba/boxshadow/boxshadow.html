Box Shadow


I will show you how to make a shadow to a box.



This is how you create the shadow, 'box-shadow: horizontaldisplacement verticaldisplacement levelofblur color;'. The 'horizontaldisplacement' is a positive or negative number, the negatives numbers move the shadow to the left and the positive numbers to the right, this moves the elements horizontaly. The 'verticaldisplacement' does the same hat does the 'horizontaldisplacement' but moves the shadow up or down, a negative number send it up and a positive one down. The 'levelofblur' determines how much blur will be in the figure, it makes the pixels expand. Finally you have to put the color of the shadow.
