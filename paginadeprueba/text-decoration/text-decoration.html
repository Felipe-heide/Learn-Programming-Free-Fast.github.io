Text-decoration


I will show you how to underline, overline or cross out the letters of a box.


You will use this declaration 'text-decorations :value;'. This are the values possible 'underline','overline','line-through', 'none' and 'underline overline'.





The 'underline overline' value will do both things. You can determine the color and the style of the lines like this 'text-decorations :value styleofline color;'.This are the possible styles of lines 'dashed','dotted','double', 'solid', and 'wavy'.
