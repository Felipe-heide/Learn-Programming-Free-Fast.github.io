I will show you how to insert an audio on your webpage.



You first write '<', then 'audio src="link of the audio" controls>' finally ''.



You can see that I used another attribute controls, I put it in order that buttons like the play button appear, without controls you can not reproduce the audio.




There is one more attribute:


autoplay loop, it plays over and over again, it is for background music.
