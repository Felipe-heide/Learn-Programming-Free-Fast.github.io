Border-radius

I will show you how to make round the angles of your box.


You have to first put the selector and between the two '{}' you will write 'border-radius : value;'. The value can be 5px, 10px etc.




You can select just one angle of the element : border-bottom-left-radius, border-bottom-right-radius, border-top-left-radius and border-top-right-radius.
